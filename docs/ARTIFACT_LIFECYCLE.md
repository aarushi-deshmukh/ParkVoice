# Artifact Lifecycle

This document defines the lifecycle for every generated artifact in the ParkVoice AI implementation path. The goal is to make all model, dataset, metric, and database artifacts explicit, traceable, and verifiable.

## Artifact Ownership Rules

1. Research artifacts may be generated by notebook or training scripts, but must be stored in a deterministic location.
2. Production inference must never depend on an artifact that is not registered in this lifecycle.
3. Any artifact consumed by inference must have a verified load and smoke-test path.
4. Artifacts with model binaries must be rebuilt whenever the training dataset or feature contract changes.

## Regeneration Policy

- Model binary artifacts are regenerated only after training output is validated.
- Metrics artifacts are regenerated alongside the model artifact they describe.
- ONNX and benchmark artifacts are regenerated only after a successful ensemble or classical ML export.
- Upload files are never reused as training data unless explicitly approved in the research layer.
